# Router Restart Procedure

## Issue
Customer is experiencing connectivity issues and basic troubleshooting should begin with a router restart.

## Symptoms
- No internet connection
- Intermittent connection
- Some devices cannot connect
- Router may appear responsive but service remains unstable

## Diagnosis
1. Ask whether the customer already restarted the router.
2. If not, ask the customer to power off the router completely.
3. Wait at least 30 seconds before turning it back on.
4. Allow a few minutes for the connection to stabilize after reboot.

## Recommended Agent Action
- Use router restart as an early troubleshooting step for general connectivity issues.
- Confirm whether the problem is resolved after reboot.
- If the issue persists, continue with line diagnosis or outage checks.

## Escalation Criteria
- Reboot does not restore service
- Connection remains unstable after restart
- Device indicators remain abnormal

## Suggested Reply
As a first step, please restart the router by turning it off completely, waiting about 30 seconds, and then turning it back on. Once the connection has stabilized, let me know whether the problem is resolved or still ongoing.
