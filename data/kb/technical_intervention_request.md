# Technical Intervention Request

## Issue
Customer issue cannot be resolved through standard troubleshooting and requires escalation to a technician or advanced support team.

## Symptoms
- Service remains unavailable after troubleshooting
- Hardware indicator suggests a persistent fault
- Repeated instability continues after reboot
- No local outage explains the problem

## Diagnosis
1. Confirm the troubleshooting already completed with the customer.
2. Verify whether a local outage has been ruled out.
3. Check whether the issue is persistent rather than intermittent.
4. Confirm contact details and customer availability if an intervention is needed.

## Recommended Agent Action
- Summarize the issue clearly in the ticket.
- List all troubleshooting steps already attempted.
- Open a technical intervention request or escalate to advanced support.
- Inform the customer of the next step and expected follow-up process.

## Escalation Criteria
- Persistent service outage with no outage detected
- Hardware indicator remains abnormal
- Prior troubleshooting did not restore service
- Problem recurs repeatedly over a short period

## Suggested Reply
Thank you for your patience. Since the issue persists after the standard troubleshooting steps and no local outage explains it, the next step is to open a technical intervention request so the problem can be investigated further.
