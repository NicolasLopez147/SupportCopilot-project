# Fiber Box Red Light

## Issue
Customer reports that the fiber box shows a red light and internet access is unavailable.

## Symptoms
- Red light on the fiber box
- No internet access
- Router restart may already have been attempted
- Connection may have stopped suddenly

## Diagnosis
1. Confirm whether the red light is stable or blinking.
2. Ask whether the customer already restarted both the fiber box and the router.
3. Check whether there is a known outage affecting the customer area.
4. If no outage is reported, suspect a line issue or hardware fault.

## Recommended Agent Action
- Confirm the troubleshooting steps already attempted by the customer.
- Check for a local outage or maintenance incident.
- If there is no outage and the red light persists, open a technical intervention request.

## Escalation Criteria
- Red light persists after reboot
- No known local outage
- No service for several hours
- Repeated incident after previous troubleshooting

## Suggested Reply
Thank you for the details. Since the fiber box is showing a red light and the equipment has already been restarted, I will first check whether there is a local outage affecting your area. If not, the next step will be to open a technical intervention request.
